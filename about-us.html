About Us


Punjab Enterprises. is a leading and experienced manufacturers of automotive parts and high performance engine parts such as Engine Valves, Valve Seat Inserts, Valve Guides, Piston Pins and Hub Bolts etc. We are committed to provide high quality and cost effective engine spare parts to our clients situated in domestic and international markets. At our manufacturing unit, We utilize latest techniques and cutting-edge technology equipment to produce best quality products which then go through various testing procedures to ensure high performance and highly accurate end products.

Our highly sophisticated infrastructure makes it possible for us to design and manufacture any valve guides as per the customers specifications in the shortest time. We are exporting to various countries including the developed and highly quality conscious markets like U.S.A & U.K besides other countries of Europe, Middle East & South East Asian Region. Our aim to provide total customer satisfaction by providing best quality products that are in compliance with international standards
